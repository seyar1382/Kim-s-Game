Introduction

How to Play

Welcome to Kim's Game, a fun and challenging memory game that will test your ability to remember and recall a series of items. Whether you're looking to improve your memory skills or just want to have some fun, Kim's Game is the perfect choice. In this game, you'll be shown a collection of items for a limited time, and then you'll need to recall as many items as possible from memory. With different difficulty levels to choose from, you can challenge yourself and see how well you can do. So get ready to put your memory to the test and have some fun playing Kim's Game!
